Schedule Overview

The study schedule divides the semester up into three modules. The three modules are:

  1. Module 1 - M1_NAME - M1_WEEKS.

    This module aims to encourage you to think about the following questions

    What are ICTs? What does ICTs and Pedagogy mean? What does it look like? Why should you use ICTs in your learning and teaching? What might it mean for your practice?
    As part of this process you will be required to start building a Personal Learning Network (PLN), will take a closer look at your curriculum documents and what they required in terms of ICTs, and be introduced to some models/frameworks that can help.

    Assignment 1 is based on this Module.

  2. Module 2 - M2_NAME - M2_WEEKS.

    This module aims to make you effective planners of units of work that have effectively integrated ICTs to enhance student learning.

    Assignment 2 is based on this Module.

  3. Module 3 - M3_NAME - M3_WEEKS.

    This module builds on your new found planning capability and aims to prepare you for Professional Experience and your future teaching. It examines a range of issues you will need to consider as you attempt to implement your planning in a real school setting. It includes you applying that and everything else you've learned in a classroom setting on Professional Experience. It also asks you to reflect on what happened on Professional Experience.

    Assignment 3 is based on Module 3 and your Professional Experience.
